Dual-currency investment is a new product feature launched by ExinOne, aiming to provide investors with more flexible and diversified investment options. By using dual-currency investment, investors can better utilize their idle funds based on their risk tolerance.


### Participation Page

In the "Invest" section (original fixed-term investment page) of ExinOne, click the "Dual-currency investment" (next to fixed-term investment) at the top to enter the dual-currency investment page.



### Specific Tutorial
After entering the dual-currency investment page, you can follow the prompts to operate the investment.


In the investment page, the "Target Price" can be modified, and the settlement date and settlement price can be selected as desired.

You can choose the appropriate settlement date and settlement price based on your risk tolerance.
