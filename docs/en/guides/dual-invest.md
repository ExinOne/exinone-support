Dual Investment is a product launched by ExinOne that offers investors more flexible and diversified investment options. With Dual Investment, you can put idle funds to work in a way that matches your risk tolerance.


### How to access

On the "Invest" page of ExinOne, tap "Dual Investment" at the top to enter the Dual Investment page.



### How to subscribe

Once on the Dual Investment page, follow the on-screen prompts to complete a subscription.

On the subscription page, the "Target Price" can be modified, and the settlement date and target price can be selected as desired.

You can choose the appropriate settlement date and target price based on your risk tolerance.
