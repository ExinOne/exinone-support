Q: What should I do if a transaction shows that my EPC balance is insufficient?

A: Some transactions require a small amount of EPC. Please make sure your wallet has enough EPC. If you don't have any EPC, please contact customer support.


Q: How do I set up an AutoInvest plan?

A: Please refer to [How to Set Up AutoInvest](./auto-invest) for AutoInvest setup.


Q: After selling tokens, how can I cash out through TIGA-Safe?

A: The USDT you receive from selling via ExinOne is stored in the "new system". You can cash it out through the TIGA-Safe (@7000104225) bot.
